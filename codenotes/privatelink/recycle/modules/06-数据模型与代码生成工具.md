## 3. 关键数据库操作映射

| 业务操作 | 影响的表 | 关键字段变更 | 代码位置 |
|----------|----------|-------------|----------|
| Endpoint 停服 | `t_vpc_endpoint` + `t_connect_info` | `close_status=1`, `end_time=now` | `db/db.go:93-118` |
| Endpoint 恢复 | `t_vpc_endpoint` + `t_connect_info` | `close_status=0`, 若 `connect_status=1` 则 `INSERT start_time=now` | `db/db.go:120-168` |
| Service 停服 | `t_vpc_endpoint` + `t_service` + `t_connect_info` | 联级 `close_status=1`, `end_time=now` | `db/db.go:170-200` |
| Service 恢复 | `t_service` + `t_vpc_endpoint` + `t_connect_info` | `close_status=0`, 批量 `INSERT start_time` | `db/db.go:202-260` |

**核心回收字段**：`close_status`（`t_vpc_endpoint` 和 `t_service` 表均含此字段），`connect_status`（仅 `t_vpc_endpoint` 表，用于恢复时判断是否重建连接记录）
